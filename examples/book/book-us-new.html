The journey with us, step-by-step

So what happens from now?

Wedding planning can be a whirlwind experience, trust us we know this by planning our own wedding! But it should be fun and exciting at the same time.

How to Book Your Wedding Videographer

01.

You reach out

Tell us about your plans, your story, and what matters most to you. If your date is available, we'll send over our full brochure and answer any questions you have.

02.

You book

Once we're a match, we secure your date with a signed agreement and booking fee. You'll then get access to our couple's guide and next steps.

03.

Get to know each other

Through casual conversations and thoughtful questionnaires, we'll learn what you value most. This helps us tailor everything to fit you, not the other way around.

04.

Your wedding day arrives

We arrive prepared, calm, and ready to capture every moment. Blending in with your guests, we'll document your day as it naturally unfolds, with minimal intrusion and maximum care.

05.

You relive it all

Your final film is delivered in a beautifully curated, private gallery. It's more than just a video. It's a memory you'll return to again and again.
